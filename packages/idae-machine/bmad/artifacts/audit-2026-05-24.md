### Warning Breakdown

1. **11× `state_referenced_locally`** — top-level `machine.store(collection)` calls in:
   - `DataList.svelte` (2)
   - `DataForm.svelte` (2)
   - `InputSelect.svelte` (1)
   - `FieldDisplay.svelte` (1)
   - `ExplorerTableInline.svelte` (5)

   > These are documented in `status.yaml` as expected. The props are strings (collection names) passed at mount time; the closure warning is a false positive because `machine.store()` is a Svelte 5 rune context call and the prop does not change after initialization in these components. **Minor** — cosmetic noise in `check` output.

2. **2× `css_unused_selector`** — `Explorer.svelte`:
   - `.explorer-toolbar`
   - `.mode-switcher`

   > The toolbar markup was likely removed or refactored during S25/S32 but the CSS blocks remain. **Minor** — dead CSS.
